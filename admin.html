The administrative body of Bangalore is which is responsible BBMP for all the governance in the city.It represents the third level of governance in city first and second are Central and state Government respectively. BBMP is run by a city council of members,including 198 corporators representing each of the wards of the city and 52 other elected representatives, consisting of members of Parliament and the state legislature. Elections to the council are held once every five years and are decided by popular vote.
